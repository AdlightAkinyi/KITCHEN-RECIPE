1 Egg

1/2 tsp Salt
1 1/2 cup Sugar
1 tsp Vanilla extract
Dairy 12 tbsp Butter, unsalted
METHOD

Melt butter and warm milk (we use the microwave)
Whisk wet ingredients together

WAFFLES

INGREDIENTS 2 cups all-purpose flour 1 teaspoon salt 4 teaspoons baking powder 2 tablespoons white sugar 2 eggs 1 ½ cups warm milk ⅓ cup butter, melted 1 teaspoon vanilla extract.
METHOD Step 1 In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
Step 2 In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter and vanilla. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended.
Step 3 Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp. Serve immediately.
